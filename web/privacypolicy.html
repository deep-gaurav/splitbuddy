BillDivide Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 22, 2024

Welcome to BillDivide! This Privacy Policy explains how BillDivide ("App", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our services. Please read this Policy carefully before using the App.

Information We Collect:

We only collect the minimum information necessary to operate the App effectively and provide you with a seamless experience. This includes:

Email Address: Used for account creation, password resets, and sending important updates about your bill splits. Name: Used for personalization and to display it within the App for group activities. We do not collect any other personal information such as phone numbers, financial information, or location data.

Authentication:

We use email OTP (one-time password) for secure account creation and authentication. The OTP is sent to your registered email address and is valid for a limited time. We do not store your OTPs on our servers.

How We Use Your Information:

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Account Creation and Management: To create your account, manage your profile, and send you important account-related information. Facilitating Bill Splitting: To allow you to create and manage bill splits, invite participants, and track settlements within the App.

Communication: To send you important updates about the App, account activity, and bill split reminders.

Improving the App: To analyze usage data and user feedback to improve the functionality and performance of the App. Information Sharing:

We do not share your personal information with any third-party companies or individuals for marketing or advertising purposes.

We may share your information under the following limited circumstances:

Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted service providers who work on our behalf to provide necessary services such as email delivery or data analytics. These service providers are contractually obligated to keep your information confidential and use it only for the purposes specified in our agreement with them. Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, such as in response to a court order or subpoena. Protecting Rights and Safety: We may share your information if we believe it is necessary to protect the rights, safety, or property of ourselves, our users, or the public. Data Security:

We take the security of your information seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

Your Choices and Control:

You have the right to access, update, and delete your personal information. You can do this by logging into your account settings or contacting us through the email address provided below.

You can also opt out of receiving certain communications from us by adjusting your notification preferences in your account settings.

Children's Privacy:

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to this Policy:

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Policy on this page.
